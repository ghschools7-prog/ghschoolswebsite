CTVET Accreditation

GH Technical University is fully accredited by the Council for Technical and Vocational Education and Training (CTVET), Ghana.

Accreditation Overview

This accreditation certifies that the university meets national standards for technical and vocational education, ensuring quality training and competency-based learning.

What This Means for Students

  • Programs are regularly reviewed and approved by CTVET to maintain excellence.
  • Graduates receive nationally recognized certificates and diplomas.
  • Enhances career opportunities in technical and vocational fields.

Quality Assurance

Our continuous commitment to quality ensures that curriculum, facilities, and staff qualifications meet CTVET requirements.
