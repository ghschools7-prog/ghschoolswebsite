Admissions Overview

GH Schools opens admissions twice each year — in January and July — for both Certificate and Diploma programs.

Applicants may purchase forms at the school's reception or apply online through our admissions portal. Completed forms should be submitted manually with all required documents.

Shortlisted applicants will be scheduled for an interview and issued Admission Letters upon successful screening.

Certificate-Level Admissions

  • Applicants with 6 WASSCE passes or higher may enter Certificate 2 / Advanced Certificate level.
  • Applicants without 6 passes or JHS holders may enter Certificate 1.
  • Applicants with no academic certificate are advised to begin with a Proficiency Course.

Diploma

  • Minimum of 3 passes (D7 or better) in relevant subjects.
  • Diploma grants access to Level 100 programs.

Required Documents

  • Completed application form (online or manual)
  • WASSCE/SSSCE/A-Level/Diploma certificates
  • Ghana Card or Birth Certificate
  • Two (2) passport-sized photos
  • Proof of payment for admission form

Our Admission Process

  1. Purchase and collect application form (online or at front desk)
  2. Fill and return completed forms with documents
  3. Schedule interview date
  4. Receive Admission Letter if successful
  5. Pay Admission & School Fees
  6. Attend Orientation
  7. Participate in Matriculation

Important Notes

  • Meeting minimum requirements does not guarantee admission.
  • All documents must be submitted before deadlines.
  • Transfer students must present academic transcripts.
