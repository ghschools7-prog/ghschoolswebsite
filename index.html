Heads of Management

Dr. Kofi Osei-Kusi

Vice-Chancellor

Dr. Osei Kusi is a life coach, a speaker, a consultant and a family man. He serves as the executive president of the Osei Kusi Foundation. He has over 18 years of experience working with young people. Many of his mentees are among business owners, middle to senior managers, leading thinkers and church leaders. He is the author of 14 books on wisdom; a columnist for the Business and Financial Times Newspaper, and a regular guest on TV and radio programs.

Mr. Leslie Addo Listowell

Rector

Mr. Leslie Listowell is a prolific and dynamic communication professional, a trained marketer and entrepreneur with over eight years of experience. He holds a Higher Diploma in Marketing from Accra Polytechnic and a BA in Marketing from Central University College. Mr. Listowell is CEO of Media Tribe Studio 233 and has worked with NET2 television, Crystal TV, GHOne TV, and Metro TV. He is also Rector and lecturer for TV Presenting at GH Schools.

Mr. Clifford Ladzekpo

Deputy Rector / Academic Director

Mr. Ladzekpo is an astute academician and multimedia expert with over 28 years in Television and Broadcast. He worked with Ghana Film Industry Corporation and was head of production for Living Word Broadcast by Pastor Mensa Otabil. He holds a Master of Philosophy in Public Health and a BA in Communication Studies. He is a life coach and counsellor and joined GH Media in 2014, rising to Deputy Rector in charge of Academics.

Mrs. Belomina Addo Listowell

Deputy Rector - Monitoring and Supervision

Mrs. Belomina Addo Listowell is an educationist with an MA in Administration in Higher Education from the University of Cape Coast. She holds a BA in Education from University of Education, Winneba, and a Diploma in Education from UCC. She joined GH Schools in 2017 and heads the Fashion and Cosmetology faculties. She also lectures Salon and Spa and handles quality assurance.

Mr. Confidence Agblobi-Tetteh

Registrar

Confidence Agblobi-Tetteh is a communications and marketing professional with experience in Multimedia, communication management, marketing, and sales. He has worked with Sunny FM, CITI FM, and Radio XYZ. He holds several diplomas and degrees including a mini MBA in Business Transformation. He is also an inspirational speaker, writer, and CEO of Best Media Consult.

Mr. Dromo Apenten

Head of Production

Mr. Apenten is a graduate of NAFTI with a degree in filmmaking, specializing in editing and camera operation. He has worked on 28 TV series, 62 TV shows, and 83 commercials. He is head of production for Media Tribe Studio 233 and teaches TV and movie production, digital video editing, camera operations, lighting, and sound.

Vincent Sackitey

Dean of Students

Vincent Sackitey holds an MA in Theatre Arts and a BA in Theatre Arts and Linguistics from University of Ghana. He has worked as TV director at Multi TV and Metro TV. He has been a linguistics tutor at University of Ghana since 2008 and is currently a lecturer at GH Media School.

Mr. Glory Boateng

Secretary / Deputy Academics

Mr. Glory Boateng holds an MA in Theatre Arts and a BA in Theatre Arts and Linguistics from University of Ghana. He has worked as TV director at Multi TV and Metro TV. He has been a linguistics tutor at University of Ghana since 2008 and is currently a lecturer at GH Media School.
