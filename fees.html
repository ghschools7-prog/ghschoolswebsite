Admission & School Fees Policy

Admission Fee: All admitted students are required to pay a one-time, non-refundable admission fee of GHC 600 within 14 days of receiving their admission letter to secure their admission slot. Students will receive student numbers upon payment.

School Fees: Students must pay 50% of tuition before registration, 70% before mid-semester to receive a 70% Fees Card, and 100% before semester end to get a Full Fees Card required for end-of-semester exams. No card = No exam.

Flexible Payment: Students with genuine challenges may request to be placed on the Flex-Digital-Accounts installment plan by contacting the finance office.
