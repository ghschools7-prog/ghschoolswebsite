Biography

Mr. Leslie Addo Listowell is a prolific and dynamic communication professional, a trained marketer, and an entrepreneur with over eight years of working experience in various roles across media, education, and business.

- Mr. Leslie Addo Listowell

His leadership philosophy centers on innovation, student-centered education, and industry collaboration. Under his guidance, GH Technical University has expanded its programs and strengthened its position as a leading technical institution in Ghana.
